<li><a href="/wiki/Millicent_Bulstrode" class="mw-redirect" title="Millicent Bulstrode">Millicent Bulstrode</a> – Slytherin student in Harry's year, a member of Umbridge's Inquisitorial Squad.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Charity_Burbage" class="mw-redirect" title="Charity Burbage">Charity Burbage</a> – Professor of <a href="/wiki/Muggle_Studies" class="mw-redirect" title="Muggle Studies">Muggle Studies</a> at Hogwarts during Harry's time at school, later killed by Voldemort.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Frank_Bryce" class="mw-redirect" title="Frank Bryce">Frank Bryce</a> – Muggle gardener for the <a href="/wiki/Riddle_family" class="mw-redirect" title="Riddle family">Riddle family</a>.</li>
</ul>

<li>Mary Cattermole – Muggle-born wife of <a href="/wiki/Reginald_Cattermole" class="mw-redirect" title="Reginald Cattermole">Reginald Cattermole</a>, saved by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter" title="Harry Potter">Harry Potter</a> from the Muggle-born Registration Commission.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Cho_Chang" class="mw-redirect" title="Cho Chang">Cho Chang</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" class="mw-redirect" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> student one year above Harry, <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> Seeker, member of <a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army" title="Dumbledore's Army">Dumbledore's Army</a> and Harry's first love interest.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Penelope_Clearwater" class="mw-redirect" title="Penelope Clearwater">Penelope Clearwater</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ravenclaw" class="mw-redirect" title="Ravenclaw">Ravenclaw</a> <a href="/wiki/Prefect" title="Prefect">prefect</a> and girlfriend of <a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" class="mw-redirect" title="Percy Weasley">Percy Weasley</a>.</li>
<li>Mrs. Cole – Matron of the orphanage where <a href="/wiki/Tom_Marvolo_Riddle" class="mw-redirect" title="Tom Marvolo Riddle">Tom Marvolo Riddle</a> lived.</li>

<li>Vincent Crabbe, Sr. – <a href="/wiki/Death_Eater" title="Death Eater">Death Eater</a>, father of <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" class="mw-redirect" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Vincent_Crabbe" class="mw-redirect" title="Vincent Crabbe">Vincent Crabbe</a> – <a href="/wiki/Slytherin" class="mw-redirect" title="Slytherin">Slytherin</a> student in Harry's year, son of <a href="/wiki/Death_Eater" title="Death Eater">Death Eater</a>, Slytherin <a href="/wiki/Quidditch" title="Quidditch">Quidditch</a> team Beater, a member of the <a href="/wiki/Inquisitorial_Squad" class="mw-redirect" title="Inquisitorial Squad">Inquisitorial Squad</a>, a friend of <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>.</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/Barty_Crouch_Jr" class="mw-redirect" title="Barty Crouch Jr">Bartemius (Barty) Crouch, Jr.</a> – <a href="/wiki/Death_Eater" title="Death Eater">Death Eater</a>, credited with facilitating the return of <a href="/wiki/Lord_Voldemort" title="Lord Voldemort">Voldemort Lord</a>, received a <a href="/wiki/Dementor%27s_Kiss" class="mw-redirect" title="Dementor's Kiss">Dementor's Kiss</a>, used <a href="/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion" class="mw-redirect" title="Polyjuice Potion">Polyjuice Potion</a> to impersonate <a href="/wiki/Alastor_Moody" class="mw-redirect" title="Alastor Moody">Alastor Moody</a>.</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/List_of_supporting_Harry_Potter_characters#Vernon_Dursley" title="List of supporting Harry Potter characters">Vernon Dursley</a> – Harry Potter's muggle uncle, married to his aunt Petunia and father of Dudley Dursley.</li>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="G">G</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: G">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>

<li><a href="/wiki/Morfin_Gaunt" class="mw-redirect" title="Morfin Gaunt">Morfin Gaunt</a> – Son of Marvolo Gaunt, brother of Merope Gaunt, uncle of Tom Marvolo Riddle, framed by his nephew for Muggle killings.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dumbledore%27s_Army#Anthony_Goldstein" title="Dumbledore's Army">Anthony Goldstein</a> – Ravenclaw student in Harry's year, member of Dumbledore's Army</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/Gregory_Goyle" class="mw-redirect" title="Gregory Goyle">Gregory Goyle</a> – Large Slytherin student in Harry's year, Slytherin Quidditch Beater, a member of the Inquisitorial Squad.</li>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="W">W</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=23" title="Edit section: W">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Arthur_Weasley" class="mw-redirect" title="Arthur Weasley">Arthur Weasley</a> – Muggle-obsessed Ministry of Magic employee. Husband of Molly Weasley, father of Bill, Charlie, Percy, Fred, George, Ron, and Ginny Weasley, member of the Order of the Phoenix</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bill_Weasley" class="mw-redirect" title="Bill Weasley">Bill Weasley</a> – Oldest son of Arthur and Molly Weasley, Gringotts employee, marries Fleur Delacour</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Charlie_Weasley" class="mw-redirect" title="Charlie Weasley">Charlie Weasley</a> – Second son of Arthur and Molly Weasley, and a member of the Order of the Phoenix, works with dragons in Romania</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley#Fred_and_George_Weasley" class="mw-redirect" title="George Weasley">Fred Weasley</a> – Son of Arthur and Molly Weasley and identical twin brother of George Weasley, a member of Dumbledore's Army, Gryffindor Quidditch Beater, co-owner of Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes, killed in an explosion caused by Augustus Rookwood in the Battle of Hogwarts</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/George_Weasley#Fred_and_George_Weasley" class="mw-redirect" title="George Weasley">George Weasley</a> – Son of Arthur and Molly Weasley and identical twin brother of Fred Weasley, member of Dumbledore's Army, Gryffindor Quidditch Beater, co-owner of Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ginny_Weasley" title="Ginny Weasley">Ginny Weasley</a> – Only daughter and youngest child of Arthur and Molly Weasley, Gryffindor student one year below Harry, Gryffindor Quidditch Seeker and Chaser, a member of Dumbledore's Army</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Molly_Weasley" class="mw-redirect" title="Molly Weasley">Molly Weasley</a> (née Prewett) – Wife of Arthur Weasley, mother of Bill, Charlie, Percy, Fred, George, Ron and Ginny Weasley, a member of the Order of the Phoenix</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Percy_Weasley" class="mw-redirect" title="Percy Weasley">Percy Weasley</a> – Third son of Arthur and Molly Weasley, Gryffindor prefect and Head Boy then Ministry of Magic employee, long estranged from his family before joining them against the Death Eaters in <i>Deathly Hallows</i></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ron_Weasley" title="Ron Weasley">Ron Weasley</a> – Best friends with Harry Potter and Hermione Granger, youngest Son of Arthur and Molly Weasley, Gryffindor Quidditch Keeper, school prefect, a member of Dumbledore's Army</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Supporting_Harry_Potter_characters#Oliver_Wood" class="mw-redirect" title="Supporting Harry Potter characters">Oliver Wood</a> – Hogwarts student, Gryffindor Quidditch Keeper and captain</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Death_Eater#Corban_Yaxley" title="Death Eater">Corban Yaxley</a> – Death Eater, Head of Magical Law Enforcement under Voldemort's regime.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Z">Z</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=25" title="Edit section: Z">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<li>Blaise Zabini – Slytherin student in Harry's year, friends with Draco Malfoy.</li>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Characters_with_no_or_unknown_surname">Characters with no or unknown surname</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=26" title="Edit section: Characters with no or unknown surname">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="A_2">A</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=27" title="Edit section: A">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Aragog" class="mw-redirect" title="Aragog">Aragog</a> – Giant spider raised by Rubeus Hagrid, lives in the Forbidden Forest.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="B_2">B</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=28" title="Edit section: B">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bane_(Harry_Potter)" class="mw-redirect" title="Bane (Harry Potter)">Bane</a> – Centaur, not very wizard-friendly.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Beedle_the_Bard" class="mw-redirect" title="Beedle the Bard">Beedle the Bard</a> – Author of several Wizarding <a href="/wiki/Fairy_tales" class="mw-redirect" title="Fairy tales">fairy tales</a>, including <i>The Tale of the Three Brothers</i></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/The_Bloody_Baron" class="mw-redirect" title="The Bloody Baron">The Bloody Baron</a> – Slytherin House ghost, suitor and killer of Helena Ravenclaw, killed himself in remorse</li>
<li>Bogrod – Goblin, Bank teller at Gringotts Wizarding Bank. Put under the Imperius curse when Harry, Ron, and Hermione broke into Gringotts.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Buckbeak" class="mw-redirect" title="Buckbeak">Buckbeak</a> (a.k.a. Witherwings) – <a href="/wiki/Hippogriff" title="Hippogriff">Hippogriff</a> belonging at various times to Rubeus Hagrid, Sirius Black and Harry Potter</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="C_2">C</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=29" title="Edit section: C">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Sir Cadogan – Armoured and somewhat mad knight occupying a painting in Hogwarts</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Crookshanks" class="mw-redirect" title="Crookshanks">Crookshanks</a> – Intelligent cat belonging to Hermione Granger.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="D_2">D</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=30" title="Edit section: D">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Dobby_(Harry_Potter)" class="mw-redirect" title="Dobby (Harry Potter)">Dobby</a> – <a href="/wiki/House-elf" class="mw-redirect" title="House-elf">House-elf</a> originally belonging to the Malfoy family, which Harry helps set free in <i>Chamber of Secrets</i>. He becomes the only paid house-elf at Hogwarts.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="E_2">E</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=31" title="Edit section: E">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Errol_(Harry_Potter)" class="mw-redirect" title="Errol (Harry Potter)">Errol</a> – <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">Owl</a> belonging to the Weasley family.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="F_2">F</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=32" title="Edit section: F">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid#Fang" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Fang</a> – <a href="/wiki/Great_Dane" title="Great Dane">Boarhound</a> belonging to Rubeus Hagrid.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/The_Fat_Friar" class="mw-redirect" title="The Fat Friar">The Fat Friar</a> – Hufflepuff House ghost.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/The_Fat_Lady" class="mw-redirect" title="The Fat Lady">The Fat Lady</a> – Witch in the painting that conceals the entrance to the Gryffindor common room.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Magical_creatures_(Harry_Potter)#Fawkes" class="mw-redirect" title="Magical creatures (Harry Potter)">Fawkes</a> – <a href="/wiki/Phoenix_(mythology)" title="Phoenix (mythology)">Phoenix</a> belonging to Albus Dumbledore, saved Harry Potter from Basilisk in the Chamber of Secrets.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Firenze_(Harry_Potter)" class="mw-redirect" title="Firenze (Harry Potter)">Firenze</a> – Centaur, professor of Divination at Hogwarts.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid#Fluffy" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Fluffy</a> – <a href="/wiki/Cerberus" title="Cerberus">Three-headed dog</a> belonging to Rubeus Hagrid, protector of the Philosopher's Stone.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="G_2">G</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=33" title="Edit section: G">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Gornuk&nbsp;– A goblin like <a href="/wiki/Griphook" class="mw-redirect" title="Griphook">Griphook</a> who worked at <a href="/wiki/Gringotts" class="mw-redirect" title="Gringotts">Gringotts</a> and later teamed up with Griphook, <a href="/wiki/Dean_Thomas_(Harry_Potter)" class="mw-redirect" title="Dean Thomas (Harry Potter)">Dean Thomas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ted_Tonks" class="mw-redirect" title="Ted Tonks">Ted Tonks</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Dirk_Cresswell" class="mw-redirect" title="Dirk Cresswell">Dirk Cresswell</a> to hide away during <i>Deathly Hallows</i></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Grawp" class="mw-redirect" title="Grawp">Grawp</a>&nbsp;– Giant, half-brother of Rubeus Hagrid</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Griphook" class="mw-redirect" title="Griphook">Griphook</a>&nbsp;– Goblin, works at Gringotts Bank which he helps Harry and his friends steal the cup from within the Lestrange vault.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="H_2">H</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=34" title="Edit section: H">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hedwig_(Harry_Potter)" class="mw-redirect" title="Hedwig (Harry Potter)">Hedwig</a> – Harry Potter's pet owl</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Magical_creatures_in_Harry_Potter#Hokey" title="Magical creatures in Harry Potter">Hokey</a> – House-elf belonging to Hepzibah Smith</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="K_2">K</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=35" title="Edit section: K">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Kreacher – House-elf belonging to the Black family, and later Harry Potter. Though at first preferring to serve the pureblood line (including <a href="/wiki/Regulus_Black" class="mw-redirect" title="Regulus Black">Regulus Black</a>, <a href="/wiki/Narcissa_Malfoy" class="mw-redirect" title="Narcissa Malfoy">Narcissa Malfoy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bellatrix_Lestrange" class="mw-redirect" title="Bellatrix Lestrange">Bellatrix Lestrange</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Draco_Malfoy" title="Draco Malfoy">Draco Malfoy</a>), he eventually likes serving under <a href="/wiki/Harry_Potter_(character)" title="Harry Potter (character)">Harry</a> as he treats him better.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="M_2">M</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=36" title="Edit section: M">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Magorian" class="mw-redirect" title="Magorian">Magorian</a> – Centaur.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Moaning_Myrtle" class="mw-redirect" title="Moaning Myrtle">Moaning Myrtle</a> – Muggle-born Ravenclaw student during Tom Marvolo Riddle's time at Hogwarts, killed by the Basilisk in a girl's bathroom, which she continues to haunt.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Supporting_Harry_Potter_characters#Auntie_Muriel" class="mw-redirect" title="Supporting Harry Potter characters">Great Aunt Muriel</a> – Great aunt of the Weasley children</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="N_2">N</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=37" title="Edit section: N">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Nagini_(Harry_Potter)" class="mw-redirect" title="Nagini (Harry Potter)">Nagini</a> – Lord Voldemort's giant pet <a href="/wiki/Snake" title="Snake">snake</a>, which does his bidding. She is one of Voldemort's Horcruxes.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Nearly_Headless_Nick" class="mw-redirect" title="Nearly Headless Nick">Nearly Headless Nick</a> – Sir Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington, the Gryffindor House ghost.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Rubeus_Hagrid#Norberta" title="Rubeus Hagrid">Norbert/Norberta</a> – <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">Dragon</a> belonging to Rubeus Hagrid, later secretly moved to the Dragon Reserve in Romania by friends of Charlie Weasley.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="P_2">P</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=38" title="Edit section: P">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Peeves" class="mw-redirect" title="Peeves">Peeves</a> – Hogwarts <a href="/wiki/Poltergeist" title="Poltergeist">poltergeist</a>, a mischievous magical being.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Pigwidgeon" class="mw-redirect" title="Pigwidgeon">Pigwidgeon</a> ("Pig") – Named by Ginny Weasley, a tiny mischievous <a href="/wiki/Owl" title="Owl">owl</a> belonging to Ron Weasley.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="R_2">R</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=39" title="Edit section: R">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Madam_Rosmerta" class="mw-redirect" title="Madam Rosmerta">Madam Rosmerta</a> – Proprietor of the Three Broomsticks in Hogsmeade</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Magical_creatures_in_Harry_Potter#Centaurs" title="Magical creatures in Harry Potter">Ronan</a> – Centaur living in the Forbidden Forest</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="S_2">S</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=40" title="Edit section: S">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Scabbers" class="mw-redirect" title="Scabbers">Scabbers</a> – Ron Weasley's pet rat in the first three stories. He is revealed at the end of <i>Prisoner of Azkaban</i> to be a wizard in animal form: <a href="/wiki/Death_Eater#Peter_Pettigrew" title="Death Eater">Peter Pettigrew</a>.</li>
<li>Scabior – Snatcher who captures Harry Potter, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger in <i>Deathly Hallows</i>.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="T_2">T</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=41" title="Edit section: T">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Travers – was a wizard and a Death Eater who escaped from Azkaban after Lord Voldemort's return.</li>
<li>Trevor – The toad belonging to Neville Longbottom that always gets lost</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="W_2">W</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_Harry_Potter_characters&amp;action=edit&amp;section=42" title="Edit section: W">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Winky – A female house elf who belonged to Bartemius Crouch Sr. until he sacked her, then worked in the Hogwarts kitchen.</li>
</ul>
